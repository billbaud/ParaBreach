ParaBreach Privacy Policy

ParaBreach is designed to be a lightweight arcade game. It does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information.

What ParaBreach collects

ParaBreach does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, payment details, or other directly identifying personal information.

Local data on your device

The game may store limited data locally on your device, such as high scores or basic gameplay preferences, so the app can remember them between sessions. This information stays on your device and is not used to identify you.

Network access

ParaBreach connects to the web only to load the game files, install updates, and support standard Progressive Web App features such as caching. The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or in-game tracking.

Hosting and platform logs

Like most websites and web-hosted apps, the hosting platform may create routine technical logs such as IP address, browser type, and request times for security, diagnostics, and traffic delivery. These logs are handled by the hosting provider under its own policies.

Children

ParaBreach is a general-audience arcade game and is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the game’s features or hosting setup change in the future.
